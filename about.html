Our Sacred History

As the Veda commands — "Swadhyaya Pravachanabhyam Na Pramaditavyam" — one must never neglect self-study and teaching. Following this sacred directive, and with the noble purpose that "Vedo'khilo Dharmamoolam" (the entire Veda is the root of Dharma), the Sree Vageeshwari Sri Satyanidhi Theertha Vidya Peetam was established.

The Vidya Peetam was inaugurated on the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashami (October 21, 2007) during the Sarvajit Samvatsara, in Nanded, Maharashtra, under the divine blessings and guidance of Sri Sri Sri 1008 Sri Satyatma Theertha Mahaswamiji of the revered Srimad Uttaradi Math.

The Peetam is dedicated not merely to studying the Vedas (Adhyayanam), but also to teaching them (Adhyapanam), thereby fulfilling both aspects of the Vedic command. This dual commitment to learning and propagation is the cornerstone of our mission for Dharma Rakshana (protection of Dharma).

What We Teach

Along with Veda Adhyayanam, the Vidya Peetam provides:

  • Introductory Sanskrit Grammar (Prathamic Samskrita Vyakarana)
  • Sri Ramayana, Mahabharata & Bhagavata — taught through interactive question-answer methodology
  • This foundation serves as a gateway for advanced studies in Vyakarana, Nyaya, and Vedanta

Community Service

While teaching Vedic knowledge to students, the Peetam also guides dharmic householders in the city, inspiring them to perform their Nitya (daily) and Naimittika (occasional) karmas with devotion and regularity.

The Peetam has also provided financial assistance to three economically underprivileged students pursuing secular education. Today, these students have secured good employment and lead dharmic lives — a testimony to the Peetam's holistic approach to service.

Locations & Journey

The Vedapatashala has been operating for 15 years in Nanded and 2 years in Nizamabad (within the Srimad Uttaradi Math premises). Having served without a permanent home, the need for a dedicated building has become the driving force behind our current construction project.

Our Spiritual Lineage

Under the Divine Blessings of Srimad Uttaradi Math

Sri Satyatma Theertha Swamiji

Established Under the Blessings of

Sri Sri Sri 1008 Sri Satyatma Theertha Mahaswamiji

The Vidya Peetam was established with the Anugraham (divine blessings) of Sri Satyatma Theertha Mahaswamiji of the revered Srimad Uttaradi Math — one of the most ancient and prestigious Madhwa institutions.

Under Swamiji's spiritual guidance, the institution was inaugurated on the sacred Vijayadashami day in 2007, carrying forward the timeless tradition of Vedic education that has sustained Sanatana Dharma through millennia.

"వేదోఽఖిలో ధర్మమూలమ్" — The entire Veda is the root of Dharma. The preservation of Vedic knowledge is our sacred duty for the protection of Dharma itself.
